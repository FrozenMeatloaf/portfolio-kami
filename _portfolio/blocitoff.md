## Explanation

My creation of a self-destructing to-do list application.  If after 7 days a users "to-do" is not completed, the program is SUPER smart enough to deem it as "not important" and  POOF...it automatically disappears like any and all of my ordinary problems! _Yes, that was sarcasm_ or was it?  

Blocitoff aided in my understanding of primary Rails fundamentals by adding exciting new gems and ideas to already known best practices.
